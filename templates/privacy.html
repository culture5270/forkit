Fork It!

Privacy Policy — Last updated March 2026

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what information Fork It! collects, how it is used, and your choices regarding that information.

1. Information We Collect

Fork It! collects minimal information to provide its service:

  • Location data — only if you grant permission. Used to find nearby restaurants. Never stored on our servers.
  • Address input — if you type an address manually. Stored only in your browser's localStorage and never sent to our servers beyond the search request.
  • Preferences — your radius, cuisine filters, and dark/light mode preference are saved in your browser's localStorage. We never see this data.
  • Account information — if you create an account, we collect your username and a securely hashed version of your password. We never store your password in plain text.
  • Saved favorites — if you save a restaurant, we store the restaurant name, address, category, and website (if available) in our database, linked to your account.
  • Comments — if you submit feedback, we collect your name (optional) and message. This is stored in our database and visible only to the site administrator.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Account credentials are used solely to authenticate you and associate your saved favorites with your account.
  • Saved favorites are used only to display your personal favorites list.
  • We do not analyze, profile, or sell your data.

3. Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not use tracking cookies or advertising trackers.
  • We do not collect your IP address beyond standard server logs.
  • We do not collect your email address or any other personal identifier.
  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties for any purpose.

4. Third-Party Services

Fork It! uses the following third-party services:

  • Foursquare Places API — provides restaurant data. Your approximate location (latitude/longitude) is sent to Foursquare to perform nearby searches. Foursquare's privacy policy applies to this data.
  • Nominatim (OpenStreetMap) — used to convert typed addresses into coordinates. Your entered address is sent to this service when you search by address.
  • Railway — hosts this application and its database. Standard server infrastructure logging may apply.

5. Cookies

Fork It! uses one strictly necessary cookie:

  • Session cookie — set only when you log in to your account. It keeps you authenticated during your visit and expires when you log out or close your browser. This cookie does not track you and contains no personal information.

We do not use advertising cookies, analytics cookies, or any third-party tracking cookies. Because we only use a strictly necessary session cookie, no consent banner is required.

6. localStorage

We use your browser's localStorage to remember your preferences (address, radius, cuisine filters, and theme). This data stays on your device and is never transmitted to our servers. You can clear it at any time through your browser settings.

7. Data Retention

Account information and saved favorites are retained until you delete your account. Feedback comments are retained until deleted by the administrator.

8. Children's Privacy

Fork It! is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page will reflect the most recent revision. Continued use of the Service constitutes acceptance of any changes.

10. Contact

If you have questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please reach out via the feedback page.
